What we do

SEFAR program offers both cash and commodity deposit in exchange for students school fees. Targeting parents from all households which involves reaching out to them before they reach out ot us. It also includes one on one calls in order to reach out to vulnerable families who are not able to access their website or services due to lack of either internet or electricity.

Soma Akiba

The Soma Akiba program allows for parents to open an education insurance account that will carter for their children's school fees both currently and in the future. This helps save the parent from both stress and worries of having to face inconviniences while either admitting their children in highschool or paying for their termly arrears. The service is helpful and affordable such that all parents regardless of class or occupation are able to deposit an amount they agree upon with the organisation so as to promote transparency.

Buyers and sellers

SEFAR program acts as an intermediary between the buyer and the seller such that a parent who lacks enough school fees or a parent who has assets can exchange it for cash which is later on adopted as their children's school fees. This means that any parent with a wish to sell their commodity can be connected to the market in order to surface their products for the exchange of cash without delay. Most of the commodities include agricultural products,handmade crafts and so much more. This will help coat a friendly business relation between the buyer and the seller.
